1973-74 Bayern Munich after winning the final against Atletico Madrid in 4-0.


The 1974 European Cup Final was contestested between Bayern Munich of West Germany and Atletico Madrid of Spain. This marked the first time in club history for both Bayern and Atletico to contest in a European Cup final. Goals in extra time from Luis Aragones and Hans-Georg Schwarzenbeck cancelled each other out, so a replay took place two days later. Bayern won the replay convincingly, with two goals each from Uli Hoeneness and Gerd Muller, giving the German side a 4-0 victory. Bayern won their first ever European Cup in the history of German Football.


This is the only European Cup / Champions League final to have been replayed.
